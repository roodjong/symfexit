Admin Guide
===========

If you are a board member, workgroup or local group contact person, or you have some administrative function; you will be given access to the admin area of the Symfexit system.

Visit the admin area by clicking the "Administration" menu button on the headers of the member area, or by going to `/admin/` on your Symfexit instance.

.. note::
   Access to the admin area does not necessarily mean you have access to all of the admin features.
   The exact features you have access to depend on your permissions, which are determined by your role in the system.
