Changing your password
----------------------

.. Nice to have a screenshot here of the change password section on the details page

On the "Details" page of the member area, scroll down to the "Change password" section to change your password.

Fill in your current password, and the new password you want to use (once more for verification), and click "Change password" to change your password.

If you forgot your password, you can reset it by going to the login page and clicking the "Forgot password?" link.
You will receive an email with instructions on how to reset your password.
